Philosophical epochs and representatives

Greeks:

The Greeks were the first civilization to create philosophy; they are the foundation of western society, created the first philosophical systems and views of life.

  • Socrates
  • Plato
  • Aristotle

Rationalism:

It states that the essential thing for acquiring knowledge is reason.

  • Descartes

Idealism:

In this view, the only thing you are sure about is your existence, so they developed how to get to know the outside world, idealist because the world is your brain and how you process it is a construct of the mind.

  • Kant
  • Hegel

Pessimism:

Pessimists say that the only thing you are sure about is your existence, so they developed how to get to know the outside world, idealist because the world is your brain and how you process it is a construct of the mind.

  • Schopenhauer

Existentialism:

In this view, man is the only thing that exists and then defines himself; man is in the world alone, your actions define you, life has no purpose you have to embrace it nonetheless you can give one to it. Expressed with anguish, nausea, and absurdism.

  • Camus
  • Sartre
