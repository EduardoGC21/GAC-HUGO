Immanuel Kant

Born in 1724 A.D. died in 1804 A.D.
Immanuel Kant was a famous German philosopher; he developed the now known philosophic era Idealism. He wrote Critique of pure reason, where, after Descartes questions reality, he develops an epistemology, because you are only sure of your existence, so you need to have the truth of the objects. Thanks to this, he developed how you get to know things and thus reality, where you only know the phenomenon, not the being by itself. Also, he tries to make a scientific, philosophical system and developed Transcendental Idealism. He wrote about politics, the illustration, the sublime, morality, and many more topics.
