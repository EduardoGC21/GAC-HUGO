G.W.F Hegel

Born in 1770 A.D. died in 1831 A.D.
Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel was a famous German philosopher. He wrote Phenomenology of Spirit, where he explains the path of the conscience’s experience until reaching the absolute. He developed the Absolutism Idealism and the philosophy of history, where history moves by the spirit’s idea reaching the Absolute.
