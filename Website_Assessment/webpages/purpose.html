Our Purpose

We aim to divulge philosophical knowledge, more specifically, great and famous quotes of some of the more transcendent philosophers of history, mostly from the philosophical epochs that involve the Greeks, Rationalist, Idealist, Pessimist, and Existentialist.

Why do we want to do this?

Because we value this wisdom and so, it needs to be preserved and transmitted to new generations to advance as a society.

This page is intended for anyone searching for wisdom; the quotes are for all, and no one, everyone, will interpret it in their way.
