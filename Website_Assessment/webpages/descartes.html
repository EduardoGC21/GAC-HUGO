Rene Descartes

Born in 1596 A.D. died in 1650 A.D.
Rene Descartes was a famous French philosopher; he developed the now known philosophic era Rationalism. He wrote Method Discourse, where he questions what he senses as effective reality. The only thing he was sure about is that he thinks, therefore he exists.

Quotes

"I think; therefore I am."

"The reading of all good books is like conversation with the finest men of past centuries."

"If you would be a real seeker after truth, it is necessary that at least once in your life you doubt, as far as possible, all things."

"Except our own thoughts, there is nothing absolutely in our power."

"Knowing supposes greater perfection than doubting."

Sapientiam, et fortitudinem
