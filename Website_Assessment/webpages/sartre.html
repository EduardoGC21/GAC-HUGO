Jean-Paul Sartre

Born in 1905 A.D. died in 1980 A.D.
Jean-Paul Sartre was a famous French philosopher. Renowned for his existentialist novels and his philosophical books, like Being and nothingness and Nausea. In existentialism, he was recognized for his Nausea view on life. In which you are conscious of your existence and the objects, and all that it implies, the end of that existence.
