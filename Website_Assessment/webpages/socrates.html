Socrates

Born in 470 B.C. died in 390 B.C.
Socrates was a famous philosopher of ancient Greece in Athens; he developed the now known way of doing philosophy “Maieutic,” he was the first of the great Athens Triad (Socrates-Plato-Aristotle). His wisdom is passed down to us through the Dialogues of Plato. He was sentenced to death because of questioning and using reason.
