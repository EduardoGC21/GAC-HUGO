Aristotle

Born in 384 B.C. died in 322 B.C.
Aristotle was a famous philosopher of ancient Greece in Athens; he developed the now known way of doing philosophy “Logic,” he was the third of the great Athens Triad (Socrates-Plato-Aristotle). He wrote Metaphysics, Nicomachean Ethics, Politics, and many more. He was the best student of Plato.
